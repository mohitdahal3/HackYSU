The Life Cycle of a Star

Scroll Down ⬇️

A Star begins at Nebula,

a vast cloud of gas and dust in space

Gravity starts to pull particles closer...

and the nebula collapses into spherical mass.

The pressure builds up in the core as it collapses

Until the pressure is so high...

Nuclear Fusion Ignites at the Core!

And it fights against gravity.

"How?" you ask.

At extreme temperatures and pressures, hydrogen atoms collide so violently that they fuse into helium, releasing immense energy.

This energy pushes outward, counteracting gravity's relentless pull.
